Interior features of the new BMW 7 Sedan

Innovative atmosphere in the cockpit

The BMW Interaction Bar, a multifunctional control element, extends throughout the entire cockpit. The multimedia experience offered in the cockpit is completed with the 14.9 inch BMW Curved Screen.

Panoramic Glass Ceiling/Sky Lounge.

Panoramic Glass Roof/Sky Lounge ensures that the car remains bright throughout the day. At night, it creates a unique ambience using dynamic lighting effects.

Executive Lounge Seats.

Sit back and enjoy: Take your place in the backseat with Executive Lounge Seats and enjoy the relaxing experience.

BMW Touch Command system integrated in the rear doors.

Thanks to two 5.5-inch touch screens elegantly integrated into the rear doors, you can easily control the Infotainment system, 31.3-inch BMW Theater Screen, automatic air conditioning, seat adjustment and more.

Bowers & Wilkins Diamond Surround Sound System.

The 1,965-watt Bowers & Wilkins Diamond Surround sound system with 40 tweeters and midrange speakers, subwoofer and shaker offers a 4D sound experience, taking your sense of hearing with you on a fascinating journey.

Executive Lounge rear passenger console

The BMW 7 Series Executive Lounge rear passenger console is a special equipment item offered on the 7 Series, BMW's luxury sedan model. This feature is designed to increase the comfort of rear seat passengers.
